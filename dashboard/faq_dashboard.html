FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

A Training Management System is a software solution designed to streamline the administration, tracking, and management of training programs within an organization.
To log in to the system, follow these steps: - Step 1: Go to the login page. - Step 2: Enter your username and password. - Step 3: Click the "Login" button. - If you encounter issues, use the password recovery option.
You can view your training history by navigating to your user dashboard and selecting the "Training History" section. This section provides details about past training modules, assignment submissions, grades, certificates, and feedback.
